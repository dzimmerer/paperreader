📖 Paper Reader

Listen to a paper

Paste an arXiv link (abs / pdf / html), any article URL, or open a local PDF.

arXiv links are resolved to the parsed HTML version when available, otherwise the PDF is parsed.

Press play and each sentence is read aloud — the spoken sentence is highlighted in light yellow and the spoken word in dark yellow.

Click any sentence to jump to it. Keyboard: Space play/pause, ←/→ previous/next sentence.
